Return & Refund Policy

Effective Date: July 10, 2025

1. Overview

As a business consulting firm, Tapper Light Solutions primarily offers digital services. Therefore, our return and refund policy is designed to ensure clarity, fairness, and transparency in our engagements.

2. Service-Based Refunds

We do not offer refunds for services that have already been delivered or substantially initiated. Each engagement begins with strategic planning and resource allocation which are non-recoverable.

3. Cancellation Policy

You may cancel a consulting agreement before the agreed project start date and receive a full refund of any advance payment. Cancellations after the project begins may be subject to partial or no refund depending on the work completed.

4. Digital Products

If you purchase any downloadable or digital material (e.g., templates, reports), all sales are final. We do not offer returns for digital goods once they’ve been accessed or downloaded.

5. Payment Disputes

If you believe a billing error has occurred, please contact us within 7 business days of the transaction date. We will review your concern and respond accordingly.

6. Refund Processing

Approved refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 7–10 business days. We are not responsible for delays caused by third-party payment processors or banks.

7. Exceptions

In rare cases of technical failure or incorrect service delivery, Tapper Light Solutions reserves the right to offer a refund or alternative compensation at our discretion.
